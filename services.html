Service 01

NRI Buyer Advisory

Buying property in India as an NRI involves layers of complexity that most advisors are simply not equipped to handle. FEMA regulations, Power of Attorney requirements, TDS obligations, repatriation planning — these are not edge cases. They are the norm for every NRI transaction.

We are built specifically for this. Our end-to-end advisory covers everything from identifying the right market and property type through to legal verification, price negotiation, and seamless transaction closure — all coordinated remotely for your convenience.

  • Initial consultation and investment goal mapping
  • City and micro-market analysis based on your goals
  • Property shortlisting and site visit coordination
  • Title verification and due diligence
  • FEMA compliance review and guidance
  • Power of Attorney advisory and coordination
  • Price negotiation and deal structuring
  • Legal coordination and registration support
  • Post-purchase documentation handover

Service 02

Auction Property Advisory

Bank and court auction properties are India's most underestimated investment category. Properties regularly sell at 20–40% below market value through a structured, bank-backed process — yet most buyers, especially NRIs, never participate because the process feels opaque.

We decode that complexity. We monitor auction listings continuously across major banks and DRTs, conduct thorough due diligence, and guide you through every step of the bidding and acquisition process — from wherever you are in the world.

  • Continuous monitoring of bank and court auction listings
  • Pre-auction due diligence and risk assessment
  • Property inspection coordination
  • EMD structuring and submission guidance
  • Auction bidding strategy and participation support
  • Post-auction payment coordination
  • Sale certificate and registration coordination
  • Possession and occupancy advisory

Service 03

Property Management for NRIs

Owning property in India while living abroad creates a unique set of challenges. Tenant sourcing, rent collection, maintenance coordination, annual inspections, utility management — every one of these requires someone trustworthy on the ground.

VittaBridge provides a comprehensive property management service for NRI-owned properties. We act as your eyes, ears and point of contact in India, ensuring your asset is protected, generating income, and being professionally maintained.

  • Tenant sourcing, screening and agreement drafting
  • Monthly rent collection and remittance coordination
  • Maintenance and repair vendor management
  • Annual property inspection with detailed reporting
  • Utility bill management
  • Property tax and local compliance coordination
  • Tenant dispute resolution
  • Regular photo and video reporting to owner
